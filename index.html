Who We are

La Satarah Limited is a women-owned and led agribusiness specializing in the sustainable production and export of premium herbs. With a strong focus on quality, consistency, and traceability, we supply fresh herbs to the EU and Middle East markets, certified under GlobalG.A.P and GRASP standards.
