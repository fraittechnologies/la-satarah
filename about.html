About La Satarah Limited

Leading sustainable agriculture through innovation, quality, and commitment to excellence

Company Overview

Excellence in sustainable herb production and export

La Satarah Limited is a premier women-owned and led agribusiness enterprise specializing in the sustainable production and export of premium herbs. Established with a vision to transform agricultural practices in Kenya, we have positioned ourselves as a trusted partner for international markets, particularly in the EU and Middle East regions.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our GlobalG.A.P and GRASP certifications, which demonstrate our adherence to the highest standards of food safety, environmental protection, and social responsibility. We operate with a strong focus on quality, consistency, and traceability, ensuring that every product we deliver meets the stringent requirements of our global partners.


With a current weekly output of 400 kg and ambitious expansion plans targeting 2,500 kg by December 2025, La Satarah Limited is on a trajectory of significant growth. Our strategic vision includes expanding to 20 acres by December 2026, positioning us as a major player in the international herb export market.

Our Mission

To cultivate and export premium, sustainably grown herbs, ensuring consistent quality, environmental stewardship, and equitable prosperity for our stakeholders.

Our Vision

To be the leading sustainable agribusiness globally,fostering food security and economic empowerment for communities.

Certifications & Standards

Meeting and exceeding international quality standards

GlobalG.A.P Certified

Our GlobalG.A.P certification demonstrates our commitment to Good Agricultural Practices, ensuring food safety, environmental protection, and worker welfare throughout our production process.

GRASP Certified

Our GRASP (GLOBALG.A.P Risk Assessment on Social Practice) certification validates our adherence to social responsibility standards, ensuring fair labor practices and worker rights.

Quality Assurance

Our comprehensive quality assurance systems ensure that every product meets international standards for freshness, purity, and safety before reaching our customers.
